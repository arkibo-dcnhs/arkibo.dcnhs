About Arkibo

"LEARN. STORE. SOAR.
WHERE LESSONS LIVE FOREVER."

Vision

To ignite a new era of academic excellence at Dapitan City National High School where no student is left behind due to a lack of resources. We envision a future where technology dissolves the barriers of distance and cost, transforming education from a burden into a seamless, empowering right for every learner.

Mission

Driven by the determination to bridge the digital divide, Arkibo exists to revolutionize the way our school community shares and masters knowledge. We are committed to:

  • Breaking down systemic barriers by providing every student, regardless of background, instant and free access to the high-quality materials they need to excel.
